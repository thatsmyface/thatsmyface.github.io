Scratch Projects!

If you have Scratch you can download these projects, for free, and play them! If you don't have Scratch you can get it ,for free, by clicking on the cat!

Airplane
Watch the airplane get saved by clicking the green flag.
Christmas
On this you can watch Santa fly by clicking the green flag!
Jet Pack Marker
Draw things for Bob to stand on and make him fly by pressing the green flag,space button, A button on the keyboard to start drawing and B to stop, and E to erase!
Fruit Punch
This is a funny animation you can play by pressing the green flag.
The Best Video Game ever
In this fun game you can play 5 different levels! Shoot as many characters as you can by pressing the green flag then use the arrows on your keyboard to move and space to shoot!

And to stop any of these projects press the red stop sign!
